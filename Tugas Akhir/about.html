HOW WE DO

“Ciao! Universe literally translates into Italian as “universo”. We choose this name as everything we do in our daily life evolves around it. Everything we do is made fresh on daily basis on our premises. By offering what’s available, what’s in season and most importantly what’s good, Chef and his team aim to deliver a humble, casual dining experience for everyone. We are a restaurant, a deli, a gelateria and a friendly social space at the same time. Whether if it’s for some authentic Italian bread and pastries from our counter or a hearty, comforting meal; something for you to cook at home, or a simple snack, we’ve got you covered. Come check us out!
